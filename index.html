What is RAG?

RAG = Retrieval + Generation

Traditional LLMs are frozen after training, leading to two critical issues:

Traditional LLM Problems

  • Outdated Knowledge
  • Hallucinations & Guesses
  • Costly & Time-Consuming Fine-Tuning

RAG Solution

  • Real-time Document Access
  • Factual, Source-Grounded Answers
  • Secure Company Data Integration
RAG gives chatbots "open-book" access to trusted documents instead of relying only on memory.
Simple Example
"What is my sick leave allowance?"

ChatGPT responds: "Typically companies offer 10-20 days..." (Generic, might be wrong)

RAG Chatbot responds: "According to your 2025 HR policy, you have 15 days of paid sick leave plus 5 emergency days." (Exact, grounded, trusted)

RAG Architecture

RAG System Architecture
This architecture ensures the LLM is NOT guessing — it's answering using actual retrieved content.
How It Works in Practice
Employee asks: "What is my sick leave allowance?"

System: Finds exact paragraph from HR_Policy_2025.pdf

LLM Response: "According to section 4.2 of the 2025 HR Policy, full-time employees receive 15 days of paid sick leave annually."

Result: No hallucination, no outdated answers — just factual, verifiable information.
